What happens when you put 40 young and creative teenagers into a movie theatre for 6 hours, loaded with popcorn and pizza, and all the design tools and mentorship they could ask for? Their mission: to create the ultimate in-theatre experience of the future.

We worked closely with Cineplex's Research & Marketing teams to understand their Gen Z audience, and engaged a local high school to align research endeavours with educational opportunities in the real world. The workshop was designed to ignite blue-sky thinking—allowing the kids to imagine to the ends of their creativity without constraints. Five unique ideas were generated by the end of the workshop, after which the Studio Bud team worked with Cineplex to extract key insights that informed each idea.
