I'm a big believer in the power of play and storytelling.

I believe that everyone is a storyteller, and that great design both tells and empowers stories to take flight. As a recovering architect, 'creative-tech'-dabbling designer, and a research-driven brand / experience strategist, my work always aims to get to the heart of the story. I utilize stakeholder engagement, data-driven research, and co-design workshops to invite people into the process to help them articulate their purpose and expression. Empowering everyone to have an active part of the process or experience—throughout an entire project lifecycle—really gets my creative heart ticking.
