Here are a few things I believe in:

  • Design.
    As someone with a foot both in Architecture (physical) and Digital Media (virtual), I'm a big believer that design is about human experiences: about holistically thinking and reshaping the way people experience the world. As a designer, my work always starts with the 'why', and that vision informs everything from the scale of a building and down to the pixel.

  • Urbanism.
    I'm always thnking about cities and urban spaces. I"m passionate for the community I live in, and how people connect with each other and their homes. My work beyond my 9-5 career is geared at building connections between communities, engaging the public in creative, collaborative play that ignites a sense of wonder, curiosity, and inspiration.

  • Storytelling.
    Technology evolves very quickly: buildings become increasingly complex through digital fabrication, and digital technologies become increasingly autonomous, ubiquitous, and responsive. But as long as your storytelling and emotional depth are intact, that's where people will connect with an experience.
