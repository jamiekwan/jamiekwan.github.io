Over the last 7 years, I've built foundations in experiential design, research, and strategy—supporting clients in the retail, technology, entertainment, and financial sectors.
Here's how my story continues to unfold.

Here's a little bit about what I do.

Let's go back to 2013, where I found a passion for using design to help people thrive.

Fresh out of school, I started my career immersing myself in community-focused design with a boutique agency. I had the opportunity to facilitate youth-engagement workshops with the East Scarborough Storefront—an adaptive re-use community hub designed in part by the neighbourhood residents and kids.

Seeing the impact of participatory design cemented my foundational beliefs in human-centered design.

I found a joy for instiling elements of discovery in my work, and rewarding curiosity through design.

I love that digital has a unique ability to prompt diverse and dynamic experiences in our day-to-day lives, especially when integrated into the built world. I pursued a Masters in Digital Media, focusing my work on human-computer interaction, kid-centered design, and multisensory experiences. I started to use a lens of storytelling—studying how narratives can impact the process and products of design as a universal language. Naturally, this landed me into the world of theme parks.

Theme Park Design taught me about the value of 'hero-centered design'.

Designing attractions (and literal fantasy worlds) showed me how heroic transformations can happen when you empower your end-user in play and imagination—design elements I think are highly transferrable to our everyday life. Today, I continue to think with a hero-centered mindset, in my current role at Gensler, exploring how to apply this to the architecture we experience everyday.



Here's a little bit about what I do.

Discovery Through Engagement

I help organizations work through big ideas and sticky problems by building a deep understanding of both quantitative and qualitative research. I design effective engagements to connect brands with their people and audiences, to discover what makes their heart tick. I've led community engagement and participatory design processes that range from middle-schoolers to senior leadership.

Synthesis & Storytelling

I love to read between the lines. I transform data into storytelling, drawing insights that reach a wide audience, informing workplace strategies, product innovation, organizational transformation, and brand positioning. I make complex ideas easy to understand.

Experience Design Roadmap

Design is not a singular endeavour—I see things from afar to understand the parts and pieces that make up a holistic design solution, whether spatial, cultural, tangible, digital, or a service. With a hero-centered mindset, I map out and storyboard the end-to-end journey, identifying and prioritizing the touchpoints that create optimal impact.

Cross-Disciplinary Management

Let's make it happen. My skills across consulting, architectural design, service design, and digital experience design gives me the language to collaborate with diverse team members on multiple teams. I pride myself in problem-solving at the blue-sky and tiny pixel scale.
