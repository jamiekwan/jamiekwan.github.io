I'm a designer / urbanist / storyteller*

*I design spaces. I design things. I tell stories. I create experiences.

I currently create spaces and experiences that ignite play, curiosity, and happiness at FORREC Ltd.

Trained in both architectural and digital media design, he works towards the creation of narrative-driven, interactive design in the museum and entertainment sector. He is an alumnus of the Digital Media Zone -- Canada's top university business and technology incubator.
