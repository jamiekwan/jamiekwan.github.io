AD ASTRA

NUIT BLANCHE TORONTO
DATE: Dec 2012 - Oct 2013
TEAM: N.ABDEL-AAL, R.GIURICICH, K.PU, S.SHAMS, H.SKONIECZNA, S.WOJTEKI
CONSULTANTS: M.LONGLADE, J.ALLEN, M.TAI
LOCATION: BATA SHOE MUSEUM

Sic itur ad astra; ‘thus you shall go to the stars’.

In the midst of the night, this piece created a serene retreat within a crowded and fast-paced nighttime event. This interactive, audio-visual art installation was exhibited as part of Scotiabank’s Nuit Blanche 2013, Toronto’s largest all-night public art festival. The room - approximately 20x20 feet in size - was filled with over 150 spherical lights, using mirrored walls to create an infinite, immersive, and ephemeral spatial experience. With a visual trompe-l'oeil, tactile interaction with select lights generates musical feedback and lighting changes to create an atmosphere akin to a dynamic night sky.
